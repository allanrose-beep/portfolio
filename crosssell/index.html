Cross-Selling Publication Support via Enago

Role: Product Manager

Company: Crimson Interactive

Brands Involved: Enago, Publication Support (PS)

Impact: +12% add-on uptake

Strategic Cross-Selling

The Context

Crimson Interactive operates multiple brands in the publishing ecosystem. Two players:

  • Enago: Academic editing and language services for researchers
  • Publication Support (PS): Journal selection, plagiarism checks, formatting, and other end-to-end publication assistance

These brands traditionally operated in silos, with separate websites and user journeys.

Problem / Opportunity

While analyzing user behavior across both platforms, I discovered a recurring pattern: many clients who used Enago for editing would later purchase services from PS separately. This exposed a clear cross-sell opportunity, one we weren’t currently capitalizing on.

Editing and proofreading services

Translation and localization services

Publication Support

The Commercials

  • Collaborated with marketing and business teams to identify overlapping services.
  • Devised a pricing strategy to make PS services more appealing when bundled with Enago.
  • For example: Plagiarism Check priced at $100 on PS was offered for $99 as an add-on within the Enago order form.
  • Repackaged key PS services as contextual add-ons and integrated them within Enago’s submission flow.

Here's how we did it

  • Designed user flows to surface relevant PS add-ons contextually.
  • Collaborated with UI/UX teams for seamless integration into the Enago order journey.
  • Aligned with marketing for conversion copy and pricing experiments.
  • Coordinated with tech and dev teams for backend tagging, service activation, and analytics setup.
