Introduction: The Leak No One Sees Coming

One of the quietly persistent challenges for marketplaces like Uber is off-platform transactions, cases where riders and drivers connect through the platform but transact outside of it afterward. This is routine in urban ecosystems where repeat interactions occur (commutes, weekly services, tutoring, etc).
